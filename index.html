ABOUT

Ours is love story infused with passion – for both our family, our roots and our gelato business. I was 19 years old and had only just arrived in Rome, when a hot blooded Roman, astride a smokey grey Ducati, cruised up beside me and asked me to the movies. Who could refuse? The rest is history. Now, 25 years later, Alessandro and I are back in Ottawa and, alongside our three children Zachary, Erica and Matthew, we’re starting the Gelateria we’ve always dreamt about. Our mission at Stella Luna Gelato Café is to make you fall helplessly, head over heels in love with gelato…and share a few laughs while we’re at it.
